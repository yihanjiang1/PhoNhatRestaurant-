Welcome to PhoNhat!

About PhoNhat!


Pho Nhat is a vietnamese eatery supplying pho and noodle options in minimalist digs punctuated by a Disney mural. We provide great food and friendly service at a affordable rate. There are also many traditional vietnamese drinks.

We are so glad that you will come and visit us !!

Recent Reviews:

Samantha L.

What a little gem!! We just moved in a mile or two down the road and discovered this pho spot on Yelp. The owner is super sweet and even recognized someone in our party, knew their name and order by memory even thought it had been almost 10 years since she had seen him!We started with shrimp spring rolls and fried egg rolls. Came out super quick, very tasty! I ordered #4 which was very flavorful, probably some of the best pho that I've had in Phoenix. Portions were crazy as is standard for a pho spot. You can't go wrong $8 pho! This place is definitely no frills, but don't pass on it. Will be coming back for date night soon!


Vladmir T.

Good pho! We ordered the $8 #1 pho Nhat to share after a breakfast smoothie earlier... It had a good broth good noodles and definitely a solid 3.75 out of five stars for quality and flavor. My mind was not blown but it was good food.Tucked in the back of this strip mall is a quiet little Vietnamese food spot.... Staff seemed friendly. Definitely worth stopping by to have a taste.
